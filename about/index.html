About us

Built for the hard
security problems.

Forecight was founded on a simple conviction: most organizations don't have a tools problem. They have an expertise and strategy problem. We exist to close that gap — with advisors who've operated at the highest levels of the discipline.

How we work

What makes Forecight
different.

01
Independent by design

We don't sell software. We're not beholden to vendor relationships that color our recommendations. Our only obligation is to your security outcome — which means you get advice shaped by your situation, not a sales quota.

02
Technical depth, executive fluency

Our advisors have operated in both worlds. We can architect a detection rule in the morning and present a board risk summary in the afternoon. That breadth is what makes our advice actionable at every layer of your organization.

03
Long relationships over quick wins

Our clients keep coming back because we treat their programs as our own. We invest in understanding your business, your constraints, and your goals — and our advice reflects that depth, not a template we pulled from a prior engagement.

04
Canadian focus

We understand the Canadian regulatory environment — PIPEDA, provincial privacy laws, sector-specific mandates — and the threat landscape facing Canadian organizations. That context shapes everything from our compliance work to our threat intelligence.

05
Accountable outcomes

We measure success by the maturity of your program, not the volume of our deliverables. Engagements have clear objectives, defined milestones, and a shared definition of what "done" looks like.

06
Honest assessment

We'll tell you when your program is in good shape and when it isn't. We'll tell you which investments will move the needle and which won't. Good security advice is sometimes uncomfortable — and we won't soften it to make it easier to sell.
