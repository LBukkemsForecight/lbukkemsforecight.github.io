Cookie Policy

Revised January 23, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Forecight Cybersecurity Inc. and its affiliates ("Forecight," "we," "us," or "our") and our service providers deploy cookies on Forecight's websites, as well as the options you have to control them. For more information on how we collect, use, and disclose personal information, please see our Privacy Policy.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of data stored in text files on your computer or other device. Cookies may be set by the site you are visiting, or by third parties such as those who serve content or provide advertising or social media integration services on the website (known as "third-party cookies"). The website or third party that sets the cookie may access it on subsequent visits, enabling websites to "remember" you and your preferences — either for a single visit (through a "session cookie") or for multiple repeat visits (through a "persistent cookie").

Both websites and emails may contain other tracking technologies such as "web beacons" or "pixels." These are typically small transparent images that allow us to gather information for similar purposes as cookies. If you disable cookies, web beacons may still load, but their functionality will be restricted.

2. How we use cookies

We use cookies for several purposes. Some cookies are necessary for technical reasons such as website security or optimization; some enable a personalized experience; some enable us to distinguish unique visitors for purposes of analyzing the effectiveness of our content and marketing; and some allow advertising networks to deliver relevant advertisements.

3. Types of cookies

  • Technical Cookies — Allow you to navigate through the website and use the different options or services available.
  • Personalization Cookies — Allow you to access our services with some general characteristics predefined based on a series of criteria.
  • Analytics Cookies — Allow us to follow and analyze the behaviour of users of the website. Information collected is used to measure activity and elaborate on user navigation profiling, with the purpose of improving and enhancing the website.
  • Advertising Cookies — Allow the management, in the most efficient way possible, of the advertising spaces on the website.
  • Behavioural Advertising Cookies — Collect data about users to track their activity and behaviour on the website. They remember user behaviour through continued observation of activity, developing a specific profile to show advertising according to information collected.

Cookie types in use

  • Necessary — Essential for the technical functioning of our website. Facilitates website security (e.g., __cfduid), optimizes the website (e.g., AWSELB), and preserves user states from page to page.
  • Analytics — Helps us collect information on how users interact with our websites, including pages visited, referring sites, and browser and device information. We use this to improve how our websites function.
  • Marketing — Used to track visitors across websites to help with our marketing activities. May be used to build user profiles to help show relevant ads.
  • Third Party / Embedded Content — Our websites may use third-party widgets and apps to enhance user experience, including integrations with social media platforms. Cookies may be set by these third parties and used by them to track your online activity for their own purposes.

4. Controlling cookies

You may restrict the use of cookies or prevent them from being set entirely. Most modern browsers allow you to change your cookie settings — find the "Help" section in your browser's toolbar for instructions. You can also manage your preferences on this site at any time using the panel.

Browser-specific guidance:

  • Microsoft Edge
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Google Chrome
  • Apple Safari

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing Google's opt-out browser add-on. If you disable cookies, some features of our service may not function correctly.

5. Contact us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please refer to our Privacy Policy for the most relevant contact details, or email us at privacy@forecight.com.
