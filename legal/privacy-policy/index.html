Privacy Policy

Revised January 23, 2026

Taking client data & privacy seriously

Cybersecurity is Forecight's core focus. We are committed to complying with applicable privacy legislation — including PIPEDA and the GDPR — and supporting our partners and clients in their own compliance efforts.

Forecight is committed to providing exceptional cybersecurity services and respects its clients' right to privacy and the request to control personal data shared. All content on Forecight's corporate website, Forecight.com, is covered by this privacy policy.

Upon your discretion and approval, Forecight may disclose personal information to its agents or subcontractors. Any designated agent or subcontractor will be obligated to use that personal information in accordance with the terms of this privacy policy. In addition to disclosures reasonably necessary for the purposes identified below, Forecight may only disclose your personal information to the extent required by law, in connection with any legal proceedings or prospective legal proceedings, and to establish, exercise, or defend its legal rights.

Information you provide us

We request information, including personal information, via forms and submissions on this site. The exact information collected depends on the purpose of the form. For example, when you complete the contact form, we may ask for your full name, company email address, work phone number, job title, organization, and region. We will indicate whether a particular field is mandatory or optional. If you choose not to provide certain information, we may not be able to provide the requested services.

Information we receive from your use of the site

We collect the following types of information automatically through cookies, device identifiers, and similar technologies. When you use or access the site, we and our service providers may use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about how you use the site. For more information, please see our Cookie Policy.

How we use your information

Forecight may use your personal information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide requested services
  • Administer and improve your experience on our website
  • Send you information you have explicitly requested
  • Personalize content and enable access to website services
  • Send statements, invoices, and marketing or event registration information where applicable

Personal information we may collect

  • Information provided through registration or contact submissions
  • Information sent to Forecight directly
  • Usage data collected to improve the site

We take precautions to prevent the loss, misuse, or alteration of personal information and secure all personal information with appropriate encryption technology.

Cross-border data transfers

Information may be stored, processed, and transferred across regions in which Forecight operates, in accordance with this privacy policy. By accessing content on our website, you agree to such cross-border transfers of personal information.

Our commitment to GDPR guidelines

We take data protection very seriously and strive to ensure that data we receive is:

  • Processed lawfully, fairly, and in a transparent manner
  • Collected only for specified and legitimate purposes
  • Limited to what is necessary and held no longer than required to operate our services
  • Processed in a manner that ensures appropriate security

We also provide features that give users control over their data, including the ability to request a copy of information we store, request erasure, and opt out of analytics and other non-essential services.

Other websites

Forecight's website may contain links to external websites. Forecight is not responsible for the privacy, content, or security practices of third-party sites.

Updating this policy

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes to Forecight's privacy and information security practices. Notification of material changes will be provided via email and/or a public notice on this site prior to the changes becoming effective.
