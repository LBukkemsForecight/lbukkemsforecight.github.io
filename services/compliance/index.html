Services / Compliance & Technical

Compliance that holds
up under scrutiny.

Regulatory frameworks exist for a reason. We help you meet them with substance — not checkbox theater. From PIPEDA to SOC 2 to sector-specific mandates, our technical depth means your compliance actually reflects your security posture.

What we do

Technical rigor,
regulatory precision.

Compliance Assessments

Gap analysis and readiness assessments against PIPEDA, NIST CSF, ISO 27001, SOC 2, CIS Controls, and sector-specific frameworks. We identify what's missing, prioritize remediation, and prepare you for audit.

Penetration Testing

Network, application, and social engineering assessments performed by certified testers. We simulate real-world attack scenarios to validate your controls before adversaries get the chance.

Vulnerability Management

Continuous identification, classification, and prioritization of vulnerabilities across your environment. We build the program, not just deliver the scan report — so you have a repeatable, defensible process.

Policy & Documentation

Security policies, standards, procedures, and guidelines that reflect how your organization actually operates. Practical documents people read and follow — not templates pulled from the internet.
